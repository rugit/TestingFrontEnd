Codenovus
“ Programming is like any other sport. You might know the rules but you have to play to learn ”
-Mark Zuckerberg

Let the coder in you emanate as we bequeath a platform to showcase your coding skills to us all. A platform that will not only test your abilities to code, but to optimize time and space as well.

It is a flagship coding competition that will be conducted in two rounds i.e. qualifying and final. The qualifying round being online can be participated from home. 20 shortlisted participants will take part in the final round that will be conducted on JEC campus at given date and time. All participants selected for the final round will be provided with certificates of merit. Winner and runners up will get cash prize plus certificates.
